When I purchase a song or album, what is the musician's cut?

Musicians receive 80% of all of their sales, whether they are bands or single artists.

Bula Bowl fans can name their price, with a set minimum.

If you want to sell your album, say for $6.00, let the Bula Bowl fan name the price. Set the minimum to $6.00 - this is like setting a tip jar on your piano; your true fans will pay more.

Add value - include your lyrics.

Lyrics are Very Important, how many times have you Googled lyrics from a song when you can’t remember the name of the song? A lot; no doubt. Having lyrics available increase the songs awareness and therefore will drive more traffic to your song. Search engines such as Google will drive more traffic to your Bula Bowl site than any other sites including Facebook simply because they are search engines.

Include Free Items when your Fans download your music.

By offering Free Items along with your download, fans will appreciate this and will give them more of an incentive to purchase your music as well as follow you on your social sites such as Twitter, Facebook etc. because they feel connected to you. Anything you give away, be sure to treat it like a special gift given to a loyal fan. Don’t be afraid to mention that downloads include, for example; special autographed photos, tour schedules and posters. You can easily add Free Items in the album editor.

Important! Utilize Pay Pal’s Micropayments and save.

Pay Pal’s standard transaction fee is 2.9% + $.30 USD. Micropayments are only 5% plus .05 per purchase. PayPal offers two methods of micropayments:

  1. Micropayments rate have 5% and 5 cents per transaction. Visit: https://micropayments.paypal-labs.com/
  2. Merchant rate pricing. Depending on your monthly volume, PayPal's rate can be as low as 1.9% + .30. Visit:
    https://cms.paypal.com/us/cgi-bin/marketingweb?cmd=_render-content&content_ID=merchant/merchant_fees

What does it cost to be in the Bula Bowl and how and when do I get paid?

  • The Bula Bowl is FREE!
  • 80% of all your music sales go directly to you.
  • Your 80% of sales revenue is sent directly to you minus the PayPal payment.

Will the Bula Bowl take any ownership rights in my music?

  • NO!
  • The Bula Bowl is non-exclusive. The Bula Bowl only requires the right to host the music you upload and sell your music. The Bula Bowl also requires the right to display all lyrics and artwork that you uploaded to the site. For more information please read the Terms of Use

What audio formats can I upload?

You can upload the following:

  • Lossless AIFF,WAV and FLAC files
  • 16 and 24 – bit samples
  • sample rates of 44.1,48,96,174.4 and 192kHz.
  • Stereo and mono

Note: There is no processing done to your file uploads. We only resample/process for downloads.

Note: Maximum file size is 291MB that you can upload.
